Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum call-out fee?

Our minimum consultation fee for doctor home visits is R800.00. This covers the doctor's time, travel, and professional consultation.

How long does registration take?

Patient registration takes minutes. Doctor registration requires credential verification which typically takes 24-48 hours.

Are doctors HPCSA registered?

Yes, all doctors on our platform are verified HPCSA professionals with valid practicing certificates.

How do I pay for consultations?

We accept card payments, instant EFT, and medical aid claims where applicable.

Is my medical information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and are fully POPIA compliant.
